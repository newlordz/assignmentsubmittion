Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an assignment?

Go to your dashboard, find the assignment you want to submit, click "Submit Assignment", upload your file, and click submit.

What file types are accepted?

We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, PPT, PPTX, ZIP, and RAR files. Maximum file size is 16MB.

How do I check my grades?

Your grades will appear in your dashboard once your lecturer has graded your submission. You'll also receive a notification.

What if I submit late?

Late submissions are marked as such, but you can still submit. Check with your lecturer about late submission policies.

How does plagiarism detection work?

Our system compares your submission with other submissions and online sources to detect potential plagiarism.
